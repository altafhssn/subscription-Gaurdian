Frequently asked questions

Is my Gmail data safe?

Absolutely. We use read-only Gmail access through Google’s official API — we never see your password. All data is encrypted and hosted in the UK. We are fully GDPR compliant and never share your data with third parties.

Does it work with UK services?

Yes — any service that sends email receipts works with Subscription Guardian. From Netflix and Spotify to your gym membership and cloud storage, if it shows up in your Gmail, we’ll find it.

Can I try without a credit card?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only pay if you decide to continue after your trial ends.

What services do you support?

We support 50+ services including Netflix, Spotify, Apple Music, Adobe Creative Cloud, Amazon Prime, Disney+, AWS, Slack, Notion, Figma, GitHub, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and many more. If it sends an email receipt, we can detect it.

How do you cancel subscriptions?

We handle the entire cancellation process. You review the subscription on your dashboard, click cancel, and we take care of the rest — no need to navigate cancellation pages or talk to support.
