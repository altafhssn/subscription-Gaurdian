1. Who we are

Subscription Guardian ("we", "us", "our") is a service that helps you find recurring charges in your Gmail inbox. We are operated by [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] registered in England and Wales (company no. [NUMBER]), with our registered office at [ADDRESS].

Our data protection contact is privacy@subscriptionguardian.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

From your Gmail (with your explicit consent)

  • Email subject lines, sender addresses, and snippets — only of messages that look like receipts or subscription notifications
  • Detected service names, amounts, currencies, and billing frequencies

We do not read personal correspondence, access drafts, send mail on your behalf, or modify anything in your inbox. Google enforces this technically through the gmail.readonly scope.

From your account

  • Your Google email address (used as your account identifier)
  • Your Google OAuth access and refresh tokens (encrypted at rest)
  • Account creation timestamp

From your use of the site

  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser, request paths) for security and abuse prevention — retained for 30 days
  • A SHA-256 hash of your IP address linked to waitlist signups (for spam prevention only — we cannot reverse this back to your IP)

We do not use third-party advertising trackers, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar.

3. Why we collect it

  • To provide the service: we read your Gmail receipts so we can show you what you're paying for.
  • To keep you signed in: we store your OAuth tokens (encrypted) so you don't have to log in for every scan.
  • To prevent abuse: we rate-limit requests and use IP hashes to catch automated signups.
  • To contact you: if you join the waitlist, we email you when we have product updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Legal basis for processing

  • Consent (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)): Gmail access, waitlist email signup.
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): processing necessary to provide you with the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): security logging, fraud prevention. You can object via the contact below.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share it only with these processors:

  • Our hosting provider ([RAILWAY / FLY.IO / similar]) — to run the service.
  • Google — because that's where the Gmail data comes from.
  • Email provider ([RESEND / POSTMARK / similar]) — only if you've opted in to email updates.

Each of these is bound by a Data Processing Agreement and processes your data only on our instructions.

6. How long we keep it

  • Account data: until you delete your account.
  • Detected subscriptions: until you delete your account or remove individual entries.
  • Server logs: 30 days.
  • Waitlist email: until you unsubscribe.

When you click Delete account, all of the above is wiped within 24 hours. We do not keep "shadow" backups for marketing.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access — get a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit how we use your data
  • Portability — get your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where consent was the legal basis

Email privacy@subscriptionguardian.co.uk with your request. We respond within 30 days.

8. Security

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using PBKDF2-derived keys (Fernet, 100,000 iterations).
  • HTTPS is enforced everywhere with HSTS preload.
  • Session cookies are HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax.
  • OAuth flow uses CSRF state parameter.
  • Source code is publicly auditable on GitHub.

If you discover a security issue, please disclose responsibly to security@subscriptionguardian.co.uk. We respond within 48 hours.

9. Contact and complaints

For any privacy question: privacy@subscriptionguardian.co.uk.

You also have the right to complain to the UK's Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113
  • Post: Information Commissioner's Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire, SK9 5AF
